About Me

I'm a business analytics enthusiast from the University of Tennessee, Knoxville, with a love for technology, numbers, and the great outdoors. Apart from my academic achievements, I've held roles that have shaped my professional skills and given me a real-world perspective on analytics. As a Graduate Teaching Assistant, I honed my analytical skills and mastered the art of providing critical feedback, while at Onsight Rock Gym, I learned the art of balancing customer satisfaction.Take, for instance, the time I worked with a team on the Hanes Brand Graduate Case Project to determine product placement in Kroger Stores using market basket analysis. Or when I took part in a hackathon and used a Gradient Boosted Model to predict Uber pickups in New York City.

One of my favorite moments was during the Clayton Homes MSBA Capstone project, where we used analytics to identify key home features driving sales and created a predictive variable to optimize their business strategy. Beyond work, I'm also a rock climber, a mountain-biker, and an electric guitar lover! So whether you're looking to chat about the latest in data science, want to swap climbing stories, or are in need of a World Cup prediction, don't hesitate to get in touch!

Multi-Digit Number Recognization

For my Deep Learning class at the University of Tennessee, I designed and implemented an innovative project focusing on multi-digit number recognition. Using TensorFlow and the SVHN dataset, I developed and trained a complex deep learning model incorporating multiple SoftMax layers. This resulted in the ability to accurately identify multi-digit numbers within images, even when presented with diverse and challenging data. The project encapsulated my proficiency in advanced problem-solving, programming, and data visualization, and the results were presented to a professional audience, demonstrating effective communication of complex data and methodologies.

Clayton Homes Capstone Project

As part of my capstone project at the University of Tennessee, I collaborated with a team to conduct a detailed analysis for Clayton Homes, a prominent player in the manufactured housing sector. We pinpointed key home features influencing sales frequency and demand, leading to the creation of a predictive variable 'DaysToSell' for upcoming housing inventory. This variable proved to be a critical asset for Clayton Homes, aiding in strategizing future home production and sales. Our project culminated in a comprehensive presentation to Clayton Homes executives, encapsulating the impact of data-driven decision-making in a real-world business context.
